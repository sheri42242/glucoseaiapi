Calculate Body Mass Index [BMI]






Your BMI will fit into one of 5 bands:

  • Below 18.5 | This is described as underweight.
  • 18.5 to 24.9 | This is described as the 'healthy range'.
  • 25 to 29.9 | This is described as obesity
  • 40 or over | This is described as severe obesity.
